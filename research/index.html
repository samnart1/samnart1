Master’s Thesis

Evaluating Circuit Breakers Under Controlled Failure Injection

Free University of Bolzano
Supervisors · Andrea Janes, Alessandro Aneggi

A 2×5 factorial study measuring how circuit-breaker configurations affect both resilience and energy consumption as failure intensity rises. Failures are injected across five intensity levels on two physical machines, and each of the ten experimental conditions is measured for performance and power draw — asking not just whether a circuit breaker helps, but what it costs.
